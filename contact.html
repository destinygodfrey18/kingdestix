Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with me

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but I typically deliver small projects (1-2 pages) in 1-2 weeks, medium projects (5-10 pages) in 3-6 weeks, and large applications in 2-6 months. I always provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you work with existing teams?

Absolutely! I'm experienced in collaborating with existing development teams, designers, and project managers. I can integrate seamlessly into your workflow and contribute to ongoing projects.

What's included in your development process?

My process includes: initial consultation, project planning, design review, development phases, testing, deployment, and post-launch support. I provide regular updates and involve you in key decisions throughout the project.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes! I offer various support packages including bug fixes, updates, security patches, and feature additions. I believe in building long-term relationships with my clients.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, and various databases. I stay current with the latest frameworks and tools to provide the best solutions for your needs.
